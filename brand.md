# Brand Card - Super_Cars

## Direction
Super_Cars là web showroom bán xe phong cách luxury editorial kết hợp Porsche-Tesla clean: tối màu, điện ảnh, premium, hiện đại, nhiều khoảng thở, nhưng vẫn rõ ràng và đủ thực dụng để chốt lead.

## Design Read
- Page kind: production web cho showroom đơn lẻ
- Audience: khách mua xe phổ thông lẫn khách mua xe sang/super car
- Vibe: luxury editorial + cinematic dark + precision engineering
- Visual family: BMW precision, Apple clarity, Porsche/Tesla-inspired restraint

## Personality
- Primary: premium, trustworthy, sharp
- Secondary: modern, cinematic, composed
- Anti-personality: noisy, discount-market, overcrowded, flashy-neon

## Typography
- Display: Geist, 600, tight tracking, high contrast headlines
- Body: Inter, 400-500, clean readable body copy
- Mono: Geist Mono

## Spacing scale
4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96

## Radius
- xs: 6px
- sm: 10px
- md: 16px
- lg: 24px
- pill: 999px
- Feel: soft but disciplined

## Elevation
- Card: soft dark shadow with subtle inner border
- Modal: deep shadow with strong backdrop separation

## Density
- balanced-to-airy
- component padding baseline: 16px

## Imagery style
- real car photography
- dark showroom lighting
- metallic reflections
- close-up detail shots for premium vehicles
- clean, bright secondary images for mainstream inventory clarity

## References (locked)
1. BMW - sharp structure, automotive authority, dark-light rhythm
2. Apple - whitespace discipline, CTA clarity, premium restraint

## UX principles
- Inventory browsing must feel premium, not like a classifieds site
- Public users should reach key actions in 2 clicks: view details, contact, book viewing
- All important CTAs stay obvious without feeling aggressive
- Admin UX can be simpler than public storefront, but must be fast and reliable

## Non-goals
- Không dùng neon gradients hoặc AI-purple glow
- Không dùng 3 card bằng nhau kiểu template landing page
- Không dùng copy khoa trương kiểu sale chợ xe
- Không nhồi quá nhiều text trong hero
- Không dùng nhiều accent color trên cùng 1 page
- Không làm marketplace nhiều người bán ở phase 1
